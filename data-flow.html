Permissions troubleshooting

Permission problems often come from the user the service runs as, wrong directory ownership or mode, or config files not readable by that user. On locked-down systems, check SELinux or AppArmor context and adjust ownership or policy as needed.

This sub-chart covers the “Permissions” node from the main troubleshooting chart. For service startup or config errors, use Services & config.
